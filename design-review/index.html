Typography

The app reads as a polished prototype, but not a premium judging product.

`Inter` plus `Manrope`, repeated uppercase labels, and repeated glass panels flatten the hierarchy instead of sharpening it.

Interaction

Several controls signal more than they actually do.

The theme toggle only flips a class with no dark or light token swap, the results toolbar buttons only log to the console, and the upload flow is missing explicit field labels and inline validation.

Layout

The flows rely on generic card stacking instead of decision-oriented structures.

Directory, scoring, and results all use the same surface treatment even though they serve different cognitive jobs: triage, evaluation, and comparison.
