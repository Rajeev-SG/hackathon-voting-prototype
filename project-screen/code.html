EcoTrack AI

by Team GreenHorizon

Real-time carbon footprint analysis using satellite imagery and computer vision to empower sustainable urban planning.

The Inspiration

Climate change remains the defining challenge of our generation. While global emission targets are set at high levels, local governments and urban planners often lack the high-resolution, real-time data needed to identify specific emission hotspots or the impact of green initiatives in their cities.

EcoTrack AI was born from the idea that we can use the "eyes in the sky" – modern satellite clusters – combined with the power of Large Vision Models to create a living map of our planet's health. We don't just measure; we visualize change over time.
